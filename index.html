Arijit Shaw

Hi! I'm a joint PhD student at Chennai Mathematical Institute, and IAI, TCG CREST. My PhD advisor is Kuldeep S. Meel of University of Toronto.

My research interest lies in building better systems for "Beyond SMT" problems - I look into designing model counting tools for different SMT constraint theories. Moreover, I look into using these tools in solving real-world problems.

Earlier, in 2019-'20, I was an intern at Meel Research Group, National University of Singapore. Even before that, I used to study Theoretical Computer Science and work on Software Verification at Chennai Mathematical Institute, India.
